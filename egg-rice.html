egg rice

Description

A simple meal you can eat every day. You can add to this any variation you like. Add kimchi and a meat to make more complete nutritionally, or some butter or furikake to make it fancy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice (cook according to rice cooker instructions)
  • 2 pasture-raised eggs
  • oil for cooking
  • salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook/heat up your rice according to package instructions.

  2. Melt cooking oil in pan set to medium heat.

  3. When oil becomes hot, crack one egg into one side, and one onto the other sie. Careful not to break the yolk if you want it to be runny.

  4. Cook until egg whites look mostly solid, or a little longer if you want the egg crispy on one side. Then, flip!

  5. Cook only ashort time on the second side, maybe 15-30 seconds.

  6. Put your egg on the rice! Add salt and pepper to tase and bon appetit. Here you can also add any topping you like - some good ones to try include green onion, soy sauce, spam, kim chi, or avocado.
