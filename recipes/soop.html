Description

Comforting and good meal to heat you up.

Ingredients

  • noodles (cooked per package instructions, cooled and drained)
  • 1 pasture-raised egg, soft-boiled (6-7 minutes)
  • oil
  • chicken bouillon
  • aromatics of choice (ginger, garlic, scallions, ginger)
  • soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Melt cooking oil in pan set to medium heat.

  2. Place aromatics in oil and toast until golden brown in color.

  3. Let oil cool, then drain solids from aromatics flavored oil.

  4. Boil enough water for soup, and add chicken bouillon and soy sauce.

  5. Assemble soup by placing drained noodles in the bowl, then egg, then broth, then aromatics oil drizzled on top. Enjoy!
