sandwich

Description

A staple in everyone's diet, and a good meal on the go.

Ingredients

  • two slices of bread
  • mayonnaise
  • meat of choice
  • sliced onions
  • if desired - lettuce, tomato, or avocado

Instructions

  1. Toast bread

  2. Add mayonnaise two one side of each slice

  3. Place sliced onions (and vegetables such as lettuce, tomato, or avocado if desired) on bread on top of mayo layer

  4. Place meat on top of vegetables

  5. Close with top piece of breead. Enjoy!
